## Mini Pizza Bagels Recipe

### Ingredients:
- **Mini Bagels**:
  - 6 mini bagels, halved
- **Sauce**:
  - 1 cup pizza sauce or marinara sauce
- **Cheese**:
  - 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- **Toppings**:
  - Sliced pepperoni
  - Sliced mushrooms
  - Diced bell peppers
  - Any other toppings you like

### Equipment Needed:
- Baking sheet
- Oven
- Spoon (for spreading sauce)
- Cheese grater (if cheese isn't pre-shredded)

### Instructions:

1. **Preheat the Oven**:
   - Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

2. **Prepare the Bagels**:
   - Slice the mini bagels in half and arrange them cut-side up on a baking sheet.

3. **Add Sauce and Toppings**:
   - Spread a spoonful of pizza sauce on each bagel half.
   - Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
   - Add your chosen toppings like pepperoni, mushrooms, and bell peppers.

4. **Bake**:
   - Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the edges of the bagels are lightly toasted.

5. **Serve**:
   - Serve the mini pizza bagels hot. They're great for snacks, parties, or a fun meal.
